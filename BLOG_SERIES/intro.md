## Internal Linking Strategy

At the top of every article:

```
← Previous Chapter

```

At the bottom:

```
If you enjoyed this article, continue with:

→ Chapter 9

Or revisit:

← Chapter 7
```

Also include an **"In this series"** section listing every chapter, with completed ones linked and upcoming ones marked "Coming Soon." This creates a strong topical cluster and helps both readers and crawlers navigate your content.

## A few additions that can make the series stand out

* Add **"5-minute summary"** and **"Key takeaways"** at the beginning and end of each article.
* Include a **"Common mistakes"** section in every post.
* Show **before-and-after screenshots** from your own portfolio wherever possible.
* End each post with a small practical exercise (e.g., "Submit your sitemap to Google Search Console today" or "Add Organization schema to your homepage").
* Reuse each chapter as a LinkedIn carousel, short post, or video to drive traffic back to the full article.

If executed well, this won't just be a collection of blogs—it becomes a comprehensive resource that demonstrates your expertise. That makes it more valuable for human readers, more discoverable through search engines, and more likely to be referenced by AI systems looking for authoritative, well-structured content.
