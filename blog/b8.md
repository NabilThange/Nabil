## **Are There Really Free Unlimited AI APIs?**

One of the most common questions among AI enthusiasts is whether truly **free and unlimited AI APIs** still exist.

The short answer is **not really**.

While many providers advertise free access, almost all of them include one or more limitations:

* Daily request limits
* Rate limits (RPM/TPM)
* Monthly quotas
* Credit-based systems
* Temporary promotional access
* Data collection for model training

However, there are still plenty of excellent free AI APIs that are more than capable of powering roleplay bots, coding assistants, research tools, and personal AI projects.

# **Can You Find a Truly Unlimited Free API?**

Unfortunately, community consensus is clear:

**Unlimited free APIs are extremely rare.**

Even providers advertising unlimited access usually implement one or more of the following:

* Rate limiting
* Daily caps
* Fair usage policies
* Temporary promotional periods
* Hidden restrictions

For most users, a generous free tier is far more realistic than unlimited access.
