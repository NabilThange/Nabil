### 2. The "Source of Truth" Strategy
To be a "reference", your website must be the **hub** of your digital identity.
*   **Update ALL Profiles**: Go to LinkedIn, GitHub, X (Twitter), Instagram, Dev.to, and Medium. 
    *   Make sure your Bio/About in all of them matches your website's bio *exactly* or very closely.
    *   **CRITICAL**: Put `https://nabil-thange.vercel.app` as the "Website" link in *every single one* of these profiles. This creates "Backlinks" that tell Google "This is the official site of Nabil Thange".

### 3. Content is Signal
Static sites die. Active sites rank.
*   **Write Blog Posts**: You have a blog section. Use it. Write 1 article a month.
    *   *Topic Idea*: "How I built a portfolio with Next.js 15"
    *   *Topic Idea*: "My experience winning HackHazards 2025"

### 4. Direct Indexing
*   **Request Indexing**: In Google Search Console, inspect your URL `https://nabil-thange.vercel.app` and click "Request Indexing". Do this whenever you make major updates.
